About Gardens

Our Mission

Gardens was founded to make sustainable, productive gardening accessible to everyone, regardless of experience level or available space.

We believe that growing your own food and creating beautiful green spaces shouldn't require endless hours of work or deep horticultural knowledge.

With operations across Europe, Asia, and New Zealand, we bring local expertise to our global community of gardeners.

Our Expertise

Our team includes horticulturists with 20+ years of experience, sustainable agriculture experts, and garden designers who understand what makes gardens thrive in different climates and conditions.
